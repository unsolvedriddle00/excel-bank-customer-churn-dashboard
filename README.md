# 📊 Bank Customer Churn Dashboard (Excel)

An interactive Excel dashboard built to analyze customer churn patterns using Microsoft Excel. This project demonstrates practical data cleaning, data transformation, KPI reporting, business analysis, and dashboard development skills.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most important business metrics in the banking industry. This dashboard analyzes customer behavior to identify the major factors contributing to customer attrition and provides actionable business insights.

The project was developed entirely in Microsoft Excel using Power Query, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer churn patterns
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Compare churn across different countries
- Evaluate customer activity and engagement
- Provide business recommendations to improve customer retention

---

## 📊 Dashboard KPIs

- Total Customers
- Total Churned Customers
- Total Retained Customers
- Customer Churn Rate

---

## 📈 Dashboard Analysis

The dashboard includes:

- Customer Churn Distribution
- Churn by Geography
- Churn by Age Group
- Churn by Credit Score
- Churn by Balance
- Churn by Salary
- Churn by Number of Products
- Churn by Credit Card Ownership
- Churn by Customer Activity
- Churn by Customer Tenure

Interactive slicers allow users to filter dashboard results dynamically.

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Around **20% of customers have churned**, indicating a significant retention challenge.
- Germany has the highest customer churn rate among all countries.
- Inactive customers are considerably more likely to leave the bank.
- Customers with high account balances represent the largest churn segment.
- Customers owning only one banking product are more likely to churn.
- Middle-aged customers contribute the highest share of customer attrition.
